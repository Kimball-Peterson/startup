The Crawler Camper. Stellar Built's LC80

Stellar Offroads has unveiled their latest project, transforming the rugged LC80 Land Cruiser into an adventurer's dream with a focus on a rooftop tent setup rather than the traditional camper buildout. This innovative design choice emphasizes mobility and simplicity, allowing enthusiasts to explore the great outdoors without the added bulk of a full camper. The LC80, known for its durability and off-road prowess, has been further enhanced with solid axles for superior traction and control in even the most challenging terrains.


The centerpiece of this build is the state-of-the-art rooftop tent, which offers a comfortable and elevated sleeping space that can be deployed in minutes. The tent is complemented by a suite of off-road accessories and modifications, including upgraded suspension and all-terrain tires, ensuring that the LC80 Cruiser is not only a mobile haven but also a capable expedition vehicle ready to tackle any adventure. This Stellar Offroads LC80 build with its rooftop tent setup represents the ultimate in off-road adventure, combining rugged functionality with the freedom to explore the unknown.
