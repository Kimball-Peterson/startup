Speed Sales is meant to be a platform for the sale of, appreciation of and community surrounding cars, trucks and motorsports. Cars across genres are welcome and sharing is encouraged. There is a main page allowing for the creation of an account, or login to an existing account. The mission of Speed Sales is to provide a way to buy, sell and discuss vehicles all in the same platform. The hope is that real time metrics, comments and a future messaging option allow for people to get a better idea of what it is they are looking at to provide a better buying experience for niche builds like offroad specific, track specific and other kinds of specialty cars that struggle to sell in the open market due to lack of understanding.
